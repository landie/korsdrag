Point to Point

  • Spaghetti integration
  • No scaling whatsoever
  • Extremely complex very fast

Hub-n-spoke

  • Less spaghetti integration
  • Somewhat scalable
  • Single point of failure

Enterprise Service Bus

  • Like hub-n-spoke
  • Scalable
  • No single point of failure
